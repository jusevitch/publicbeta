About#

I’m a full-time Technical Staff at MIT Lincoln Laboratory performing research on autonomous systems. I comleted my PhD in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Michigan. You can read more about my work at my personal website.

I find a vast range of topics interesting. I love learning, and I’m constantly finding a huge list of resources and tools that I find interesting or useful.

Personal Life#

I follow Jesus Christ. Applying the principles He taught has helped me become a better man. My church is the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints.

In my spare time you’ll probably catch me spending time with my wife and daughters, volunteering at my church, running, learning new programming languages, or composing music. I also love an occasional round of disc golf 1.
